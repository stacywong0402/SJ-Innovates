Meet the Team

making San José a better place to live, work and raise a family



Want to be a Mayoral Fellow?

Khanh Duy Russo

Director of Strategic Partnerships


Khanh Duy Russo works to leverage partnerships, innovation, community resources and investments for creative, public problem solving. Prior to joining the mayor's office, he worked at Cisco, where he guided the global strategy for technology-based solutions addressing critical human needs. He holds a master’s degree in Public Policy and Management from Carnegie Mellon University, a bachelor’s degree in Political Science and Religious Studies from Santa Clara University, and was a Coro Northern California Public Affairs Fellow.

Shireen Santosham

Chief Innovation Officer


Shireen Santosham leads efforts to build the "smart city" vision and strategy for San Jose as chief innovation officer for Mayor Sam Liccardo. Previously, as director for the Mobile for Development group at GSMA, she aimed to close the digital divide through providing mobile technology and internet access in the developing world. She has extensive experience in innovative initiatives, including at McKinsey & Company, international NGOs, and as an impact investor at Vulcan Capital. Shireen holds a M.B.A. and an M.P.A in International Development from Harvard and is a term member at the Council on Foreign Relations.

Ingrid Holguin

Policy Advisor


Ingrid Holguin brought over 20 years of experience in economic and community development to Mayor Sam Liccardo’s office as a policy advisor in early 2015, where she focuses on building financial capabilities for individuals and institutions. Prior, she served as director of Savings Programs for the Opportunity Fund, a community development financial institution, and as a program officer for the Ford Foundation in Mexico City. She has worked in philanthropies, impact investing, strategic planning, and program evaluation and design. Ingrid holds an MBA from the University of California, Berkeley, and a B.A. in Economics and Management from Ball State University.

Sachin Jain

Policy Advisor


Sachin Jain works on understanding and applying the awesome power of data and technology to make government more open and effective — redefining citizen engagement and revitalizing local democracy. Prior to joining the mayor’s office, he worked for UC President Janet Napolitano’s Executive Office, California Governor Brown, the United States Senate, the White House, and numerous education reform nonprofits. Sachin holds a bachelor’s degree in Environmental Policy and a master’s of Public Policy from UC Berkeley. In his spare time, he loves to travel, dabble in photography, watch Cal sports, pretend he’s decent at cooking and explore the outdoors.

Mayoral Fellows

Dima Khoury

He regularly spoke at large trade, industry and company conferences, including Intel Developers Forum, IBM DB2 and IBM CICS annual conferences and worked with senior government officials, including ambassadors and ministers.

Gisela Bushey

Bob Rao

Nicholas Almedia
